How AI Can Prevent Stampedes in India

Public Safety | PinchTree Team | 2026

Stampedes are one of the biggest public safety threats in India. Festivals, rallies, religious gatherings, and stadium events can turn deadly when crowd movement becomes uncontrollable.

Why Stampedes Happen

  • Sudden panic wave or rumor
  • Overcrowded gates
  • Unplanned crowd flow
  • Security incident

How AI Solves It

AI can analyze CCTV feeds in real time, detect abnormal movement patterns, panic waves, or sudden running. This allows authorities to act before the stampede starts.

Why Threat Detection Systems Matter in Convoy Safety

Defence Sector | PinchTree Team | 2026

Key Threats

  • Weapons and sharp objects detection

  • Road barricade detection
